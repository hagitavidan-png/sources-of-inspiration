Visual Personal Journal · Lesson 0.2

Your life is not separate
from your art.

It is your source.

Pause

Think about a small moment
from your life.

Not a big event. A quiet, ordinary moment that stayed with you.

Focus

Choose one moment
that stayed with you.

Emotion

What did you feel?

Not what happened. What the moment felt like — inside.

Colour

If this feeling had a colour —
what is it?

Shape

What shape does it have?

Not a literal shape. A feeling of shape.

Start

Start drawing
the feeling,
not the story.

Use the colour and shape you chose.
No objects. No narrative. Just the feeling.

Personal Layer

Add something
only you understand.

A hidden word. A mark. A detail no one will notice unless you tell them.

Deepen

Make it more personal.
Less perfect.

The places where it breaks or becomes uncertain
— those are the most honest parts.

Reflection

What did you discover?

✓ Saved

Your life is something
you create from.

Every moment you've lived is material.
You don't need to look anywhere else.
