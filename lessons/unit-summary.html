Unit Summary

You finished a unit.
But what stays?

During the lessons
you paused,
looked,
and drew from yourself.

Now we don't draw.
We just look.

Return

Look at one drawing
you created.

Not to judge —
but to see.

What is happening
in your drawing?

Notice

Which feeling
appears there?

Where in the drawing
do you see it?

It doesn't have to be clear.
But it's there.

Reflect

What did you discover
about yourself?

Sometimes a drawing
reveals something
we didn't know how to say.

This unit

You didn't just learn
to draw.
You learned to listen.

Take with you

What are you taking
with you from this unit?

Leave a moment
for yourself.

The drawing
doesn't end here.
It continues with you.

Unit complete

You're not only drawing.
You are seeing.
